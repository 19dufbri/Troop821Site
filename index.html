Troop 821 Solon, Ohio

Boy scout troop 821 offers an open and inviting enviroment for scouts to teach, learn, lead, and experience teamwork. Our troop is made up of friends and neighbors and would love to welcome you. We meet every monday and have monthly campouts for you to enjoy. Come check us out at the link below and see what troop 821 can do for you!

Leadership

Troop 821 is a boy led troop, meaning that the scouts plan and create the adjenda for meetings and campouts. Scouts can get elected into leadership positions and are then responsable for being a leader and helping the troop run smoothly. These leadership positions along with other parts of troop work help scouts deleop leadership skills needed later in life. This command stucture allows the troop to run smoothly and helps everybody get a say in what we do.

Campouts

Each month the scouts plan and complete a campout or special event. These include our Klondike competition, week long summer camp, backpacking trips and even going scuba diving at florida sea base. We also go to high adventure camps like Seabase and Philmont where the more experienced scouts can work together to complete more challenging goals. Through these campouts the scouts learn skills that can be carried into their normal lives.

Meetings

We meet once a week to plan our campouts and learn new skills. At our meetings we have meritbadge sessions, requirement learning, and team building activites, each week is different. These meetings help the scouts gather together to discuss plans and earn the requirements needed to earn the various ranks in scouts. We also have fun meetings where we have patrol competitions where each patrol cometes to be the best, building leadership and teamwork in the process.

Leadership

Scouts are a great way for young men to learn the important leadership skills needed later in life. The scouts offer this oppertunity through leadership positions and having a troop led by the scouts. Every six months the scouts elect several people to lead the troop and be responsable for jobs such as quartermaster and troop guide. Scouts also learn leadership through the planning of our campouts and other troop activites. Everybody has a chnace to lead a group and will learn lots through the experiences they gain.
