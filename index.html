Troop 821 Solon, Ohio

Troop 821 is a boy led troop, allowing the scouts to gain the experience needed to be leaders in the real world. We have all agee groups in our troop and one of our core ideas is to have the more experienced scouts to teach the younger scouts. Learn more today.

Leadership

The troop offers many leadership oppertunities to scouts. Every six months we elect officers who make decisions and lead the troop. The leaders plan and execute the campouts and meetings.

Campouts

We go on monthy campouuts to various locations and even go on high adventure camps at Seabase and Philmont. These campouts are picked by the scouts and each month offers a new oppertunity.

Meetings

We have weekly meetings where we work on planning, rank advancement, fun competitions, or merit badges. Our meetings are planned and run by scouts who come up with fun and interesting ideas.

Leadership

Scouts are a great way for young men to learn the important leadership skills needed later in life. The scouts offer this oppertunity through leadership positions and having a troop led by the scouts. Every six months the scouts elect several people to lead the troop and be responsable for jobs such as quartermaster and troop guide. Scouts also learn leadership through the planning of our campouts and other troop activites. Everybody has a chnace to lead a group and will learn lots through the experiences they gain.
